What is Back Fire 031?

BACK FIRE 031(Pty) Ltd.(hereafter known as BACK FIRE 031) is a 2019 established Business that operates in the Security Industry.The Business is located in Mpumalanga Township and provides security services and skills development centre in this areas as well as surrounding areas in the KZN province. Back fire031 Security and Training is a security and training company that aims to provide unparalleled security solutions and training services to individuals,private and government.Our company is located in a strategic location like local townships that allows us to reach our target market easily,and our team of experts possesses extensive experience and expertise in the field of security and training.

Our Vision

BACK FIRE 031 has a vision to be the leading private security services provider and skill development centre in South Africa by providing world class safety and protection services.The business aims to impact the economy of South Africa,through job opportunities and people empowerment.

Our Mission

BACK FIRE 031's mission is to provide affordable and quality security services and skills development to its clients.

Integrity

We believe in upholding the highest ethical standards in all aspects of our operations, ensuring that our clients receive trustworthy and reliable security services.
